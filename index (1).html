About TNS Label Records

TNS Label Records was born from the vision of Kusa Boyo and a friend of Kusa long ago. In 2020, Kusa brought the label to life with a mission: to showcase the incredible talent of Ethiopian artists, especially those from Gambella, and to discover hidden gems who deserve to shine on the global stage.

Although the label has signed multiple artists over time, only a few remain due to past challenges. Under the leadership of CEO Kusa Boyo, TNS continues to nurture and elevate artists like Solido, King Siedee, and Koal Bwoi.
